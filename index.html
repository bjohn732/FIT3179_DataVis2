Academy Awards (Oscar)
- celebrating film professionals and excellence

With a history dating back to the year 1929 where the first Oscar was given, it is the oldest among all academy awards. A popular theory of how the award's nickname came about would be when Director of the Academy, Margaret Herrick, took a glance at the statuette and said that it resembled her Uncle Oscar.



Television Academy Awards (Emmy) - celebrating TV professionals and excellence

This award is dedicated to TV shows or programs made either on Daytime or Primetime. The origin of its nickname "Emmy" is a term known as "immy" which was used for the image orthicon tube commonly found in early TV cameras.

a look at trending titles for each continent. This data was taken from flixpatrol.com for titles trending from around Week 12 till Week 37 of the year 2020.

Different Place, Different Taste

The scatterplot chart provides a overview of how trending titles vary by
location. At first glance, we can see that North America takes the lead in producing trending shows and films; these include Lucifer, Umbrella Academy and Project Power.

The 'Hallyu' Wave

With the recent K-drama and K-pop craze taking the world by storm, there has been an increase in trending titles from Asia. It's Okay to Not Be Okay takes the spot as the top-rated drama which trended in the majority of 2020.
